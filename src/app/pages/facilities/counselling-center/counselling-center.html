Counselling Facilities

Our dedicated Counselling Centre provides personal and group counselling for academic, emotional, behavioural and career-related guidance. Students receive structured support in a safe and confidential environment.

The Centre conducts psychological assessments including:

IQ Test – Designed to measure the cognitive abilities of students.
Personality & Psychoprofile Test-Administered primarily to students in 7th and 8th grades, these tests help in understanding individual personality traits and behavioral tendencies.
Career Test – Specifically for 10th-grade students, this test aids in identifying suitable career paths based on their interests and aptitudes.
Study Habit Test-This test evaluates students’ study patterns and techniques, offering insights for improvement.
Emotional Intelligence (EQ) Assessment-This evaluates a student’s ability to understand and manage emotions effectively.
16 PF Personality Test-This evaluates a student’s ability to understand and manage emotions effectively.

The Centre operates every Tuesday and Friday within the school premises, providing inclusive support for students, parents and teachers.
