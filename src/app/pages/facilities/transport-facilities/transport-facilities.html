Transport Facilities

To ensure students from distant places attend school regularly and punctually, transport arrangements have been established. This initiative aims to overcome barriers to attendance caused by distance, enabling students to access education conveniently and reliably.


Van facility is available for students.
Parents should consult the school transport in-charge for necessary details. Van facility is not mandatory.
