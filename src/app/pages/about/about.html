Vivekanand Pratishthan English Medium School

Vivekanand Pratishthan English Medium School, established in June 2008, is a leading institution under the Vivekanand Pratishthan Foundation, offering education from Nursery to 10th standard. The school blends traditional values with modern teaching practices, aiming to provide world-class education that ensures both academic excellence and holistic development.

The school's philosophy is rooted in four core values—Knowledge, Respect, Social Responsibility, and Creativity. These values shape every aspect of learning, encouraging critical thinking, empathy, community engagement, and innovative problem-solving.

In addition, the school promotes nationalism, patriotism, and social sensitivity, helping students develop pride in their culture while maintaining a global outlook. Various activities and programs nurture awareness, compassion, and responsible citizenship.

With a strong focus on quality education and teamwork, the school fosters a collaborative learning environment where teachers and students work together to grow, improve, and excel. Through this balanced approach, the institution aims to shape academically strong, creative, and socially responsible individuals who contribute positively to society.

Education is meant to make life better, help personal growth, enhance thinking abilities, and lead to good morals.

-Dr. Avinash Ramachandra Acharya

Principal's Message

Greetings from Vivekanand Pratisthan English Medium School! It gives me great pride to welcome you to this esteemed institution. Along with academic excellence, we instill strong Indian cultural values and encourage participation in sports and extracurricular activities. We believe that a balance of knowledge, character, and physical fitness leads to complete development.

We, at Vivekians, work on the overall development of the child since we ardently believe in the holistic development of our learners… in mind, body and spirit… beyond the acquisition of knowledge and the scoring of grades. I consider it not just my duty but also my passion to provide all Vivekians with opportunities to be on such a path.

We focus on developing essential 21st-century skills such as critical thinking, creativity, problem-solving, and digital literacy—ensuring our students are well prepared for the rapidly evolving global world. Our educational approach is aligned with the National Education Policy (NEP), promoting independent thinking, innovation, and a multidisciplinary learning experience.

Our impressive academic results in past years are a testimony to the hard work teachers are putting in to ensure our children excel and learn in a conducive, supportive and motivating environment.

We see our school parents as equal partners in this endeavor and encourage them to engage excitedly in their child's journey here in close collaboration with teachers.

It gives me immense pleasure to lead a school that nurtures young minds, builds strong character, and empowers students to become confident, responsible citizens of tomorrow. Our dedicated team of teachers work relentlessly to provide a balanced education that combines values with innovation, tradition with technology and discipline with freedom.




Warm regards,
Principal,
