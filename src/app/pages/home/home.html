About Our Institution

Shaping Future Leaders Since 2008

Vivekanand Pratishthan English Medium School, established in June 2008, is committed to delivering world-class education from Nursery to 10th standard. We focus on holistic development, nurturing creativity, responsibility, and excellence in every learner.

Our core values include knowledge, respect, social responsibility, teamwork, patriotism, and quality education — building strong character alongside academic achievement.
